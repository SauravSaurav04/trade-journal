Welcome to Trade Tracker

Trade Tracker is your personal trading journal and analytics dashboard. Built for both new and experienced traders, it empowers you to log every trade, gain insights from your history, and make data-informed decisions. Say goodbye to scattered notes and spreadsheets — Trade Tracker keeps everything organized, secure, and actionable.

Key Features

📌 Log Your Trades

Easily record trade entries, exits, instruments, strategies, and more — all in one clean interface.

📊 Performance Analytics

Understand your trading behavior with win/loss stats, P&L charts, and trend visualizations.

📝 Reflect & Improve

Jot down personal notes and insights for each trade to refine your approach and mindset.

🔐 Secure & Private

Your trade data stays safe and private, accessible only by you through secure authentication.
